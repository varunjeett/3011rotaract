Greetings!

At Foodcham, our primary goal is to bring a change in the food ordering system of a restaurant and bringing better customer experience to the very niche of the food Industry, i.e. fine dining restaurants by replacing the already running Physical Menus.

Since digitization is a reflection of the future, pushing the Menu and orders of a restaurant online fetches room for analysis and improvement in the user experience. The primary goal is giving a command over the digital system.

Our Products

Digitized User Menu

This product is a mere replacement for a physical menu and manual workforce in coming times. Giving the users a command over the food they order and a better experience of the restaurant menu digitally.


Where the detailed description helps the customer better understand the food they are eating, it provides immense data and upselling opportunities for the restaurants.

Waiter Menu

This product is mainly to decrease ordering time in a restaurant, where a steward saves time in running the lengths. The orders directly pass to the kitchen through digital Foodcham tablet software.


Removing any extra manual intervention, this helps speed up and accurate the orders and hence enhanced customer experience. 

Why Foodcham?

We stand out at a personalized product facility for the restaurant. Over a basic flow template, the restaurant owner can personalize our software based on his ambiance, mood or interest. Likewise, the user has the advantage to customize what the restaurant has defined in the menu. Also, up-selling of items and data analytics for the restaurant are our unique advantages.


While the customer gets a digitized enhanced experience, the restaurants get a bulk of user data and behavior, which in turn help us recommend them menu items as per their taste buds on their fingertips. While we get item-specific feedback from customers, the users get to know more about what they are eating.
