Shukran- Thanksgiving Ceremony of DRR PHF RTR Anmol Chawla

1st July'18 / BB Verma Auditorium, Asia Pacific Institute of Management, Jasola, Delhi.

The Rota year 2017-18 came to an end on a happy note with the Thanksgiving ceremony of DRR Anmol Chawla and his team.

Many dignitaries graced the occasion with their presence. Rotaract Clubs of 3011 also joined us enthusiastically and went home with numerous awards. All the clubs had shown enormous efforts throughout the year which were,hence, proved fruitful.

We hope the Rota year 2018-19 will be more fulfilling and will prove to be the best one.

Salaam-e-Watan - Independence Day Celebrations

13th August'18 / Faculty of Arts, Delhi University

The event commenced with the flag hoisting and felicitations of Major Gen (retd) Satbir Singh and the mother of 2nd Lieutenant Puneet Nath Dutt, Ashok Chakra (P).

A Mime act named "A BALLOON" on 'Drug Independence' was also performed by actor Sumit Arora.

Kite Flying & Rotary Showcase and Branding of different Projects was also done.

The event was graced by the benign presence of District Governor Rtn. Ravi Choudhary and IPDG Rtn. Narasimhan Subramanian.

Envision - District Training Assembly

31st July'18 / K.R. Mangalam School, Vikas Puri, New Delhi

RID 3011 successfully organised the District Training Assembly - ENVISION 2017. A great number of rotaractors from different clubs under RID 3011 showed up for the Assembly which made the training sessions very interactive and informative altogether.

A special token of thanks to Guest Of Honours of the event DGN Suresh Bhasin and Mrs Meenu Kanwar, Principal of K.R. Mangalam School for their benign presence and motivating everyone present there with their exhilarating speech.
