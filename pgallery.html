Dawn- The Installation Ceremony of DRR PHF RTR Ashima Agarwal and her team.

29th July'18 / New Delhi Institute of Management, Tughlakabad, Delhi

The ceremony was graced by benign presence of esteemed Rotarians, RSAMDIO team, RI Committee Member, Council of Governors and DRRs from RID 3011 , 15 plus DRRs from different districts of 2018-19 Batch and Presidents from various clubs along with members.

Congratulations to Rtr. PHF. Ashima Agarwal for being installed as First Lady DRR of RID 3011 in the 51st tenure of Rotaract history and heartiest congratulations to each and every member to be officially installed as DAC 2018-19 Council Members.

President/ Secretary Elect Training Seminar 4.0

17th June'18 / Faridabad, Haryana.

The session was graced by our District Governor Rtn. Vinay Bhatia who explained about Rotary. The training seminar on competent leadership was taken by Rtn. Nikoonz Agarwal, District Rotaract Chair.

PDRR Harsh Sirohi, District Trainer and IPDRR Anmol Chawla took sessions on Rotary Six Areas of Focus, International Forum respectively and Rotaract Guidelines.

Our DRR Rtr Ashima Agarwal revealed RID 3011 theme of the year and half year plan.

Rtr Yaamini Thareja, District Secretary Reporting introduced the Point System and Rtr Ankit Gupta, District Secretary Admin guided the Elects about rotary.org. It was the day full of learning and fellowship among all the rotaractors.
