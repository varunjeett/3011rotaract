President/ Secretary Elect Training Seminar 4.0

17th June'18 / Faridabad, Haryana.

The session was graced by our District Governor Rtn. Vinay Bhatia who explained about Rotary. The training seminar on competent leadership was taken by Rtn. Nikoonz Agarwal, District Rotaract Chair.

PDRR Harsh Sirohi, District Trainer and IPDRR Anmol Chawla took sessions on Rotary Six Areas of Focus, International Forum respectively and Rotaract Guidelines.

Our DRR Rtr Ashima Agarwal revealed RID 3011 theme of the year and half year plan.

Rtr Yaamini Thareja, District Secretary Reporting introduced the Point System and Rtr Ankit Gupta, District Secretary Admin guided the Elects about rotary.org. It was the day full of learning and fellowship among all the rotaractors.

DOTS - District Officers Training Seminar

16th June'18 / Faridabad, Haryana.

The session was graced by our District Governor Rtn. Vinay Bhatia who explained about Rotary. The training seminar on competent leadership was taken by Rtn. Prateek and Rtn. Nikoonz followed by session on importance of Collaboration of Rotaract and Interact by Rtn. Gaurav Ahuja.

Rtr. Shaswat Desai took session on relevance of Rotaract Beyond 3011 and shared his experience of his Rotaract journey.

Rtr. Harsh Sirohi and Rtr. Anmol Chawla took sessions on Rotary SIx Areas of Focus and International Forum respectively. Our DRR Rtr Ashima Agarwal revealed RID 3011 theme of the year and half year plan.

It was followed by the panel discussion from 2017-18 Team, we had Rtr Shreya Snehi, Rtr Navdeep Singh Oberoi, Rtr Ankit Lakhina and Rtr Yaamini Thareja who discussed their Past experience.
